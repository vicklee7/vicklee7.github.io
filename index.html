STATEMENT OF FAITH

  • We believe in the Holy Scriptures. 2 Timothy 3:16-17

  • We believe in Eternal God. Matthew 26:19

  • We believe in the Fall of man. Romans 5;12

  • We believe in the Plan of Redemption. John 3:16

  • We believe in Salvation by Grace. Ephesians 2:8

  • We believe in Repentance and Acceptance of the Lord Jesus Christ as a pre-requisite for Heaven. 1 John 1:9

  • We believe in the New Birth as a foundation of our Christian Life. John 3:3

  • We believe in daily Christian Life as a guarantee of preparedness to enter Heaven. Hebrews 6:1

  • We believe in Water Baptism and the Lord Supper. Matthew 28:19; 2 Corinthians 11:28

  • We believe in the Baptism of the Holy Spirit with the Speaking in a New Tongue as a sign. Acts 2:4

  • We believe in the Spirit Filled Life. Galatians 5:16-25

  • We believe in the Gift and Fruits of the Holy Spirit. 1 Corinthians 12:1-11; Galatians 5:25

  • We believe in Moderation ( in our dressing and disposition). Philippians 4:5

  • We believe in Divine Healing as taught and practiced in the Holy Scriptures. James 5:14-16

  • We believe in the Payment of Tithes and Offerings as a must for every heaven conscious Christians. Malachi 3:10
  • We believe in Church Government or Administration. Romans 13:1-5

  • We believe in the Judgment Seat of Christ ( Greek word

  • We believe in the Second Coming of the Lord Jesus Christ. 1 Thessalonian 4:16-17

  • We believe in Church Attendance. Acts 16:5;  

  • We believe in Evangelism as a means of  'Bema' ), Eternal Reward of faithful Christians. 2 Corinthians 5:10

  • We believe in Heaven as a place of eternal bliss(Home). John 14:2-3; 1 Corinthians 2:9

  • We believe Hell and Lake of Fire as a place of eternal punishment. Revelation 20:14-15


You will get to know us by going through the Statement of Faith. Please, feel free to read it carefully!
